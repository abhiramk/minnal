Reporting bugs / Requesting Enhancements

Create an issue for reporting bugs or feature enhancement requests under https://github.com/minnal/minnal/issues. The issues will be prioritized and be taken up in the coming milestone.

Contributing to minnal

Fork minnal to your account and submit a pull request with your changes. The smaller the changes, the easier it is for us to review and merge to master
